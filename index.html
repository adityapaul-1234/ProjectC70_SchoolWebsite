What's New

  • Technology Lab

    In an effort to be more technological and digital, we have taken a decision to re-build our Technology Lab so that our students learn more about the future because the future is Technology! We are planning to have 200 desktops and 100 laptops for students to have practicals, learn coding, complete their collection sheets and projects!

    Lately, many parents and teachers have been wanting our school to have the facility and to teach swimming to our children. We have taken this initiative to fulfill the demand of all our parents and students so that St. Xavier' Family can be together!
